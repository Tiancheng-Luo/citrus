Links

The primary resource for all things to do with parsing expressions can be found at MIT.

pdos.csail.mit.edu/~baford/packrat

A useful summary of parsing expression grammars can be found on Wikipedia as well.

en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parsing_expression_grammar

Citrus draws inspiration from another Ruby library for writing parsing expression grammars, Treetop. While Citrus’ syntax is similar to that of Treetop, it’s not identical. The link is included here for those who may wish to explore an alternative implementation.

treetop.rubyforge.org
